Cake Supermarket

I have built this project to showcase the various full-stack technologies that I have learnt so far. I am passionate about baking, so I thought it would be a good idea to build a social platform for bakers to share their work. Users can sign-up (using Passport JS) to create profiles for their bakery, view other bakeries and also leave comments. Users can update or delete their own profile/comments (using middleware).

Cow Bull Game

Instructions:

  • Guess the 4-digit number. Eg: 9436
  • You earn a cow point for every correct digit and position. Eg:2416 = 2 Cows
  • You earn a bull point for every correct digit but wrong position. Eg: 4261 = 2 bulls
  • Or say, you entered:1439 = 2 Cows & 1 Bull
  • But be careful! You have limited attempts to get it right.
  • HAVE FUN!
